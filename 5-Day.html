About Classroom Cravings

Classroom Cravings is a 5-day meal plan meant for students who may be struggling to find healthy and fairly priced meals for school. Each day provides a new meal with vegetarian and non-vegetarian options, this also includes a fruit, side and a drink. Every meal is meant to be nutritious and filling whilst focusing on still being affordable. The thought process behind this was to help reduce the levels of obisety amoungst teenagers in South africa

5-Day Healthy School Lunch Plan

Monday
  • Main: Rigatoni pasta with guanciale in bolognese sauce topped with mozzarella.
  • Vegetarian: Rigatoni pasta with bolognese sauce.
  • Fruit: Mango slices.
  • Drink: Still or sparkling water.

Nutritional Value

  • High carbohydrates for energy.
  • Protein supports growth.
  • Mango provides vitamin C.
  • Water supports hydration.

Tuesday
  • Main: Brown rice bowl with grilled chicken or beans.
  • Fruit: Banana or apple.
  • Side: Yogurt with granola.
  • Drink: Apple or mango juice.

Nutritional Value

  • Fiber from brown rice.
  • Protein from chicken/beans.
  • Fruit provides vitamins.
  • Calcium from yogurt.

Wednesday
  • Main: Whole-wheat wrap with beef or beans, vegetables, avocado and tahini.
  • Sides: Corn and green beans.
  • Drink: Smoothies.

Nutritional Value

  • Fiber from whole wheat.
  • Healthy fats from avocado.
  • Vegetables provide nutrients.

Thursday
  • Main: Whole-wheat sandwich with turkey or chicken.
  • Side: Green salad.
  • Fruit: Grapes.
  • Drink: Tropical juice.

Nutritional Value

  • Lean protein supports muscles.
  • Fiber improves digestion.
  • Fruit provides antioxidants.

Friday
  • Main: Tuna, egg or hummus sandwich.
  • Side: Cherry tomatoes.
  • Fruit: Grapefruit.
  • Drink: Sugar-free flavored water.

Nutritional Value

  • Protein and healthy fats.
  • Low sugar hydration.
  • Vitamins from fruit and vegetables.
